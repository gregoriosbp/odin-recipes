Greek Chicken Thighs

Description

If you're looking for something easy, authentic, and delicious, that might last you through the week, look no further than this classic Greek recipe. Using nothing but a pack of chicken thighs, a sack of golden potatoes, and some olive oil and spices, transform your kitchen into your giagia's! This is a dish that's so simple, yet delicious, it's sure to impress anyone, even that date you've been looking forward to seeing.
