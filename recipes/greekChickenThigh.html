Greek Chicken Thighs

Description

If you're looking for something easy, authentic, and delicious, that might last you through the week, look no further than this classic Greek recipe. Using nothing but a pack of chicken thighs, a sack of golden potatoes, and some olive oil and spices, transform your kitchen into your giagia's! This is a dish that's so simple, yet delicious, it's sure to impress anyone, even that date you've been looking forward to seeing.

Serves between 4-6 people

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Packs of Bone-In, Skin-On Chicken Thighs
  • 2 Lemons
  • 1 Sack of Golden Potatoes
  • Chicken Bone Broth
  • Olive Oil
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cut golden potatoes into quarter pieces
  3. Salt and pepper your chicken thighs
  4. Add enough olive oil to coat your potatoes and chicken (about 1 cup)
  5. Combine salt, pepper, oregano, and the juice of your lemons to taste into the olive oil
  6. Place potatoes in the bottom of a glass or ceramic, high-walled baking pan
  7. Place chicken on top of potatoes
  8. Cover chicken and potatoes in olive oil and spice mixture
  9. Add about 1/2 cups of bone broth to the bottom of your pan
  10. Place pan in oven for ~25 minutes
  11. When chicken skin is crispy and meat is cooked through, remove chicken from pan and set aside
  12. Turn broiler on high, and leave potatoes in for another 2-3 minutes
