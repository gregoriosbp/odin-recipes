Lamb Youvetsi w/ Orzo

Description

This classic is simple, delicious, and will transport you home with every bite. With saucy orzo and tender lamb, all braised together in a bright tomato sauce, this dish is a prime comfort food and one that will surely satisfy even the most hungry of guests!

Serves about 3-5 people

Ingredients

  • Boneless Leg of Lamb
  • Orzo
  • Tomato Paste
  • 1 Can of Chopped Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Red Wine
  • Olive Oil
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Bay Leaves

Steps

  1. Finely chop your red onions
  2. Cut lamb into small chunks and season with salt and pepper
  3. Toss red onions into a high walled pan with olive oil and saute on low heat until soft
  4. Increase heat to about medium, then throw in lamb and sear on all sides until a crust has formed
  5. Dissolve tomato paste in red wine and add mixture into your pan. Reduce wine
  6. Add tomatoes, about a cup of water, your bay lea(ves)f, sugar, and a pinch of salt
  7. Turn heat down and allow to lightly simmer with the lid on for about 45 minutes
  8. Saute Orzo in a separate pan with some olive oil until golden brown
  9. Put orzo and meat with sauce in an oven tray, cover with aluminum foil, and bake at 180 degrees celsius for about 30 minutes
  10. After the 30 minutes, remove foil and return to oven for 15 more minutes. If there doesn't seem to be much moisture, don't be afraid to add water
  11. Serve while hot and enjoy with grated kefalotyri over top
