Lamb Youvetsi w/ Orzo

Description

This classic is simple, delicious, and will transport you home with every bite. With saucy orzo and tender lamb, all braised together in a bright tomato sauce, this dish is a prime comfort food and one that will surely satisfy even the most hungry of guests!

Ingredients

  • Leg of Lamb
  • Orzo
  • Tomato Paste
  • 1 Can of Chopped Tomatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Red Wine
